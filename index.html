INVESTOR PITCH: THE ENGINEERING HUB

A tinkering workshop based in the outskirts of Nairobi to bring a portfolio of projects to life and popularize RnD among the Afrikan youth.

The Vision

We are building an "Innovation Engine" that bridges the gap between digital design and industrial reality. By integrating mechanical engineering with firmware and electronics, we capture high-value markets in automation and industrial repair.

Market Focus

Our star focus is Business & Industrial Equipment—redesigning and repairing commercial machinery where precision is a premium. We also target Medical Tech (prosthetics) and High-End Niche Consumer products like drones and wearables.

Self-Sustaining Growth

We leverage our facility to build our own tools. We manufacture our own CNC routers and injection molders, drastically lowering the cost of acquisition as we scale our RnD capabilities.

Strategic Goal: Achieve 20% annual sales growth while maintaining a 50% machine utilization rate.
